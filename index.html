About the App

The idea for this app was born when I needed to drink water every 15 minutes. I wanted something simple — set 15 minutes, press Start, and let it ring forever until I stop it. That’s how Repti Repeat Timer Alarm was created.

Whether you need reminders every 4 hours for meals, every hour for tablets, every 30 minutes to move, or even a single-use 10‑minute timer to boil eggs — this app adapts to you.
